📌 Purpose

Students practice identifying ending sounds. The target word is read aloud 3 times automatically — students listen to how the word ends before choosing their answer, just like the real IREAD-3 assessment.

📐 Standard: RF.3.3.a

Use letter-sound knowledge of consonant blends and digraphs to decode phonetically regular words, independent of context.

🎤 How Audio Works

  • The word is spoken 3 times automatically when each question loads. Three dots ● ● ● track the count.
  • Tap 🔊 to hear it again as many times as needed.
  • The word is hidden — students click 👁 Show Word to reveal it.

🎛️ Teacher Toggles

  • 👁 Show Word Button — turn OFF to remove the hint option.
  • 🎨 Ending Hint — when ON, the ending letters are underlined in red after revealing.
